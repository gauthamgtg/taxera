Frequently asked questions

What documents do I need to share each month? keyboard_arrow_down
We keep it simple: just your sales invoices, purchase bills, and any credit notes. You can upload them as you generate them throughout the month or all at once. We'll provide a friendly checklist so you never miss a thing.
How do you ensure I get all my tax credits? keyboard_arrow_down
Our platform automatically matches your purchase records with the data uploaded by your suppliers (GSTR-2B). If there's a discrepancy, our experts investigate personally and help you follow up with vendors to ensure you save every bit of tax possible.
Is my financial information handled securely? keyboard_arrow_down
Trust is the core of our service. We use bank-standard encryption and strict data privacy protocols. Your information is only accessible by your dedicated compliance team and used exclusively for your tax filings.
